What is The Madrasah?

The Madrasah is a institution that offers education to the children in their community. They offer lessons on a number of things, such as the practices and traditions of the Islam. Their mission is to educate the children and give them the chance to learn and pursue their education despite of their circumstances. Most of the children that they teach in their community are from difficult background, which hinders them from properly pursuing their education because of the circumstances they are in. Some of them even go to work before going to school because they have to help their parents support their family. The Madrasah does their best to help them in their education with the tuition of 250 per family. No matter how many members of the family will attend school, the tuition will stay as 250. This is great--as sending multiple members in the family to school usually costs lots. The Madrasah also offers and provides learning materials to their students. These school's students also mostly range from kindergarten to grade six students.

The Madrasah's Mission

It is the mission of Madrasah to prepare the children belonging to the islamic faith to become assiduous and committed muslims. Through the development of their social, moral, intellectual, and religious education through a traditional madrasah method, the program seeks to empower the muslim youth to pursue beneficial endeavors for themselves, and their families. This program was intended to balance out the lack of educational materials for such a method. The programs that are included in this mission are teaching the Arabic language both in reading and writing, guidance to the social and religious role of an islamic child, and outreach programs.

Their Rich History

Arab missionaries and Malay explorers brought Islamic education to the Philippines, and Madrasah education was mostly done in the teacher's home, although sometimes in the masjid or mosque to learn Arabic reading and writing as means to read the Qur'an. It is thought to correspond with the rise and spread of Islam. A missionary and his religious efforts help strengthen the growing Islamic community and spread Islamic teachings. Another Arab missionary rose to prominence, becoming a sultan and overseeing the construction of mosques and the foundation of madaris.

Their Organizers

Madrasah is a community-based institution that provides Muslim children the basic education they will need. This institution lets the children be educated so they can have knowledge on the Arabic language and Islam. The organizers also encourage peace and understanding with the Muslims and Christians. This community-based institution is currently seeking help to make their education system better for the children to have a good experience with their education. They are accepting school supplies, materials donation and programs all of which can give the children a proper and well experience with their education.

Their Beneficiares

The beneficiaries of this organization are the Muslim youth in the community. As the Marasah is a community-based institution, the ones who benefit from this institution are these young Islamic children. They are provided for with quality education in the Arabic language and Islam. They are also provided with educational materials and provisions for daily supplies.

Their Benefactors

The benefactors of the program are the organizers of the Madrasah Program. They provide the school and the teachers with the resources needed and many other things.An example of what they provide are daily provisions and school materials. They also accept donations so that they can provide more for the school, so anybody can become a benefactor to the community.
