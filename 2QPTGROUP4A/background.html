Problem Identification

This project was made to help the Madrasah. After researching on our partner institution, we learned that the Madrasah has been facing many problems, particularly the problems of a lack of funds and educators. The lack of educators can impact the institution negatively in many ways, especially in the quality of the education they give to the students in the Madrasah. Because they are understaffed in this aspect, the teachers will have to carry the burden of doing the work preparing lessons and taking care of many students. They might get overworked and feel demotivated to teach properly, hindering the quality of the lessons they provide to the students. And because of the lack of funds, they encounter difficulty to provide learning materials and other needed equipment to the students.

Proposed Intervention of the Project Proposal

With this project, we can help ease the problems of the Madrasah in our own way. We are able to help them in teaching the children and providing some of the necessities they need to teach them. This institution is doing a wonderful thing by helping the children in their community, and our project is something we made in hope to help them carry out their advocacy as much as we possibly can.

Background on Our Project

The main focus of this project is to help the Madrasah with their mission of educating the children in their community. During our implementation, we will be volunteering to help the Madrasah teach the children every week. This is so that we can lighten the load and help them with their problem. We will also be selling munchkins to people in our school and using the profit from it to buy school materials for the children of Madrasah. Any funds we have that we are not able to liquidate will be given to the Madrasah.

This project also helps promote the education of the children of Madrasah. The project aims to sell at least 500 pieces of munchkins. With the money we make from selling the munchkins, we will be buying school materials for the students in the Madrasah. Because they have these learning materials, their learning experience will be much better. Our budget for this project is 784 PHP, which will be used to purchase the materials and ingredients needed in selling and making the munchkins.

Project Rationale

This project is very important because it helps increase the number of educators readily available to teach the students in the Madrasah. As we have found out, the Madrasah is suffering from a shortage of teachers and educators. This is a serious problem, because that would mean that they might not be able to teach all the students properly or effectively. With our project, we are able to help out by volunteering to teach the students as much as we can so that the Madrasah does not have to worry about struggling to find people who are able to teach their students. They will not have to spend anything as well as we will be handling expenses of our tutoring services with the money we create from selling munchkins. So, our project is important because it helps ease the Madrasah with one of the problems they are coping with, and also ensures that no new issues or problems will pop up for them while we are implementing this project.

Project Objectives

We aim to help the Madrasah with their issue of a lack of educators by volunteering to teach the Madrasah students.
The specific objectives of this project are:
1. Compute all expenses made when making munchkins, and pricing each fairly.
2. Create munchkins and sell 100 or more pieces.
3. Reach target amount of earnings to use for buying and preparing learning materials.
4. Buy, acquire, and prepare all needed materials for the tutoring services.
5. Tutor the children in the Madrasah.

Project Outcomes

The primary outcome is to help the Madrasah by volunteers who will help them teach the children.
1. Enough munchkins should be sold to be able to buy school materials for the children in Madrasah.
2.Worksheets and lesson materials should be properly prepared for tutoring.
3.The project success will be measured with the following indicators: amount of munchkins sold, amount of school material that can be bought, and the amount of students who have found our services helpful.
